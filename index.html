About me

A future educator,
rooted in purpose.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), building a strong foundation in teaching methodologies, educational psychology, and classroom management.

My academic journey has been shaped by a deep belief that education is one of the most powerful tools for personal growth and social development. I am learning to create engaging lesson plans, understand diverse learning needs, and foster active participation among students.

I am particularly drawn to building inclusive learning environments — spaces where every student feels seen, valued, and motivated. A good teacher, I believe, does not just impart knowledge; they inspire confidence, creativity, and critical thinking.

"My goal is to create an inclusive and engaging classroom where every student feels valued and motivated to learn."
